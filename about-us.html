Established in 2014, PT Anomali Lintas Cakrawala (ALC Group) is a company providing Information Technology (IT) by adopting the latest technology to meet the needs of a very dynamic market. With almost eight years of experience, we have over 100 clients from various industries. ALC Group has evolved into a modern, integrated group of companies with four main core businesses: Information and Technology, Agribusiness, Energy, and Creative Industry.

ALC group has a proven history of being successful early in new markets and tackling complex projects. Utilizing advanced technology and toiler business strategies, ALC Group has been helping many customers accelerate their technology ambitions.

The most important value in running ALC's business is trust from client and partner. ALC Group with a vision and mission-ready to become a big company in the regional area.

Spirit entrepreneurship to contribution in society and the environment, ALC Group will never stop developing many businesses in every sector. Being competitive but not to be a competitor, we are always open for collaboration and cooperation. We create a great ecosystem, as we always says “Talent wins Games, but Teamwork and Intelligence win Championships”
